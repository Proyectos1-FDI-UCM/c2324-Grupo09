Descripción

Join Seraphine, a fallen angel on their escape through hell in a 2D platformer game.

Fight enemies and recover Seraphine powers to fight increasingly difficult challenges.

Unlock powerful ablities

Jump Kick

Kick on air to be able to change your movement direction quickly

When kicking a wall or an enemy Seraphine will jump on the opposite direction

Pogo

Dive vertically to fall quickly to the ground

If Seraphine hits an enemy while falling they will do a high jump

When falling to the ground whith the dive jumping shortly before will realize a High Jump

Slide

Grounded burst of movement

Jumping while sliding will realize a long Jump

Wall run

Wallrunning allows Seraphine to reduce significantly their fallspeed

Beware that wallrunning for a long period of time will cause Seraphine forces to vanish and end up falling

Fight dangerous enemies

Imp

The weakest demon in hell still desires your death

Blue imp

Stronger than his communist counterpart

Hitting him a second time while launch him as a projectile that will destroy walls or damage another fiends
