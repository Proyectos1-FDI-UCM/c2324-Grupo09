Join the fallen angel Seraphine on their escape through hell in a 2D platformer game.

Vanquish enemies and recover Seraphine's powers to face challenges of mounting difficulty.

Unlock powerful ablities

Jump Kick

Deliver a kick while airborne to quickly change your movement direction.

Upon colliding with an enemy or wall, Seraphine will rebound in the opposite direction.

Pogo

Plunge downward to reach even greater heights.

Upon colliding with an enemy, Seraphine will rebound upward.

Upon landing, Seraphine can follow up with an enhanced jump.

Slide

Gain a sudden burst of movement while grounded.

Utilize your momentum to reach even further beyond.

Wall run

Run across unique walls to retain height.

Be mindful of running too long.

Fight fearsome foes.

Imp

The weakest of all devils would still wish upon your demise.

Blue Imp

More than a stepping stone; a tool.

Bene

A ruthless enemy that descends from above.

Hin

Surreptitious threats lie in wait and strike.

Naha

The infernal wyrm, as infamous as it is indifferent.
